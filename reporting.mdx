**Reporting** gives you an overview of your support workload, allowing you to analyze trends, identify improvement areas, and answer questions like:

- Are we prioritizing support requests correctly?
- Which product areas are creating most of our support volume?
- Which companies are we speaking with most frequently?
- Are we becoming more efficient?
- And much more
